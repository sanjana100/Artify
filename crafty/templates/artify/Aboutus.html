About Us


ARTIFY was launched with a mindset of uplifting art work of unheard voices in INDIA. If ARTIFY was to be described in a single word, ‘Irreverent’ would be it. Not the insolent, sacrilegious or rude variety, but more the cheeky anti-authoritarian kind. This is evident in the brand’s edgy, provocative and tongue-in-cheek advertising. A brand that questions everything, pushes boundaries, constantly re-invents and never strays near the beaten path. Artify, a Fair Trade Organization, are helping to spread the concepts and benefits of Fair Trading in India and abroad. Thousands of artisans and producer groups depend on ARTIFY for assistance throughout India. ARTIFY Association is a society formed with the objective of preserving crafts of India through marketing them abroad and movinf towards development. To apply the surplus generated from the business to fulfill the objective of the association and engage in acts of charitable nature.
